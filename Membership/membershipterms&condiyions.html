Membership Terms & Conditions

At Sweat, all our memberships are sold in a contract-free, month-to-month auto-recurring subscription format. By purchasing a membership with Sweat, we agree to provide you with access to the services listed for your membership in exchange for payment.

Should your payment be declined, we will automatically send you an invoice for payment. If your payment is delinquent by more than (7) seven days, you acknowledge that your access to Sweat FXBG will be suspended until you have paid your account balance in full and provide a card on file with Sweat through our payment processing system for your future invoices.

If you would like to cancel your subscription for any reason, please message the studio through our website chat platform or in-app (no social media messages or personal messages, please) or call our studio and we will gladly cancel your membership. If your cancellation request is received after your membership has renewed for the month, the cancellation will take effect prior to the next month's charge. You will have membership privileges through the remainder of your paid term. Membership charges are non-refundable
